Who we are

A modern agency based in the heart of Dublin City. We represent Actors for film, television and stage; and Models for editorial, catalogue and show,
both in Ireland and internationally. We provide casting solutions and cast management for advertising, commercial and digital, including Child Models and Family Casting.

Our story

Runway Models Agency is the longest established International Model Agency in Ireland. Stablished in 1973, by Jose Parra Runway Models has proudly been ireland's leading medelling agency.
Over the years, in its quest for perfection, the agency expanded internationally increasing our portfolio of models and business substantially.
Runway Models went from strength to strength in all areas, including the International arena where we forged strong relationships with agencies across the globe
becoming the first port of call for clients coming to Ireland to work and source new talent. We now have a wide representation of talent dotted around the globe.

What we do

Runway Models provide a complete service supplying Models, Photographers, Fashion Stylists, Make-up & Hair Artists, Actors, Commercial talent, Influencers and Personalities,
to a broad and ever expanding list of clients. Spotting, encouraging and developing talent is at the core of our agencies ethos, the mainstay of our success.

Become a model

If you are 5’8″ or above (Women) / 6' or above (Men) then please send us one headshot, one profile, one smiling, and one full length photo
with your contact details, age, height, bust, waist and hip measurements to info@runwaymodelsagency.com
We do not require professional photos, camera phone digitals are fine. Please NO FILTERS, NO TAN, NO MAKEUP, NO SELFIES.
Natural photos where we can see your skin and hair clearly. We do our best to reply to all submissions but due to the volume we receive it is not always feasible.

Runway Models connects clients globally with the best of Ireland’s distinctive model talent, leading creative artists and extensive production opportunities. Our innovative rosters champion authentically engaging voices and showcase fresh perspectives reflective of a continuously evolving social and visual culture.
